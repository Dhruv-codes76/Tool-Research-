Automated Transcription at the Edge

Whisper-Base-X represents the next evolution in low-latency speech-to-text processing. By implementing a sparse attention mechanism and deep quantization, the model achieves a 40% reduction in inference time compared to previous iterations without sacrificing lexical precision.

Our research team focused on edge-case linguistic anomalies—specifically non-standard dialects and specialized technical jargon in aerospace and medical domains. The result is a robust, production-ready core that operates entirely on-premise.
